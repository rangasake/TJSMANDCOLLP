Consultancy

Overview

TJSM aims to understand the framework under which the organization works and provides business consultancy to companies. Based on this understanding, TJSM focuses to bring about validation of business plans and implement successful business strategies.

In an ever changing and complex business environment, organizations are constantly striving to innovate, optimize business practices, grow and protect their businesses. It therefore becomes essential for organizations to collaborate with professionals who can offer valuable insights in order to achieve efficiency in terms of improvement in operations; capitalize on opportunities in the given regulatory framework and help deliver better working outcomes.

A compliance audit is carried out to have a comprehensive review of an organization's adherence to regulatory guidelines. Compliance audits could consist of determining whether accounting personnel are following the approaches prescribed with the aid of the company, examining contractual agreements with bankers and other lenders, compliance with stock exchange directives, central financial institution directives or governmental rules.

Accounting outsourcing is a cost-effective way for organizations to improve the finance and accounting function. The key benefits of outsourcing include fractional use of finance and accounting professionals, enhanced processes and technologies, and stronger compliance measures.
Outsourcing enables organizations to scale resources up and down as needed with no obligation to pay salaries or benefit costs. Additionally, it alleviates the burden of hiring, training and maintaining accounting staff.

Comparative analysis is a way to identify the competitive positions and operating results of a business over a defined period. We assess company’s financial statements using percentages, ratios and amounts when making financial comparative analysis. By analyzing financial comparatives, businesses are able to pinpoint significant trends and project future trends with the identification of considerable or abnormal changes.

Financial institutions around the world are outsourcing and offshoring core processes to reduce annual costs, increase capabilities and improve quality. Compliance outsourcing helps to gain in efficiency and quality through leveraging structured processes and also seamless execution of end-to-end processes, from compliance assessment through corrective action.

Corporate financial restructuring involves restructuring the assets and liabilities of corporations, including their debt-to-equity structures, in line with their cash-flow needs to promote efficiency, support growth, and maximize the value to shareholders, creditors and other stakeholders.

Strategic planning is one of the most important elements to get right for a company aiming for success. It is not simply about setting out what a company does, and it does not dictate exactly what a company will be doing in five years’ time, it will allow the company to function correctly and for growth to be sustainable and long lasting. Drawing on our expert business insight, we assist companies invent a strategy that will help them sustain a crisp competitive edge.

For each organization, transactions represent significant business events. It is therefore of great importance to implement a tailored approach and involve experienced and dedicated specialists to bring deals safely through to conclusion. Our Transaction Services team helps clients identify risks in transactions, so they can make informed strategic decisions with confidence. Our team works closely with key stakeholders & advisors, including lawyers & financiers, to provide a synergistic approach to navigating your transactions.

The IND-AS are basically standards that have been harmonized with the IFRS to make reporting by Indian companies more globally accessible. IND- AS involve application of several new and complex concepts, which require a significant level of judgment and estimation, accompanied by detailed qualitative and quantitative disclosures. TJSM provides following services in IND –AS implementation.
  • Assistance in identifying / deciding new policies / procedures required to be implemented of under IND –AS.
  • Assistance in implementation of identified changes for convergence to IND- AS.
  • Training your staff on key IND- AS concepts and requirements.
  • d. Assistance in compilation of financial statements under IND- AS.

    • A payroll audit is an analysis of a company's payroll processes to ensure accuracy. Payroll audits examine things like the business's active employees, pay rates, wages, and tax withholdings. You should conduct a payroll audit at least once per year to verify your process is up-to-date and legally compliant. If you fail to audit your payroll process, your small business can end up doling out excess money, breaking employment laws, and remitting incorrect tax amounts.

    Forensic audit essentially involves examination and assessment of a company’s or an individual's financial information that can be used as evidence in court of law. The objective of a forensic audit is to analyze financial transactions for the purposes of identification of financial frauds (asset -theft frauds).

    A Follow up Audit is an audit designed to evaluate the effectiveness of a corrective action that is officially closed after all of the recommendations have been recommended for closure through the follow-up audit process. The Follow up Audit is the evaluation of the adequacy, effectiveness, and timeliness of actions taken by management or responsible organization on reported observations and recommendations, including those made by auditors.
