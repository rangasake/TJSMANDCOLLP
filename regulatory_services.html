Regulatory Services

With years of tax experience, the team at TJSM is well positioned to offer end-to-end managed solutions. 



Registrations / Licenses of business under various laws applicable to the entity are provided in a cost effective and hassle freeway. We provide various Registrations / Licenses like Food License (FSSAI), Trade License, Labor License, MSME Registration Etc.

Our team shall provide the required advisory for the start-up / new businesses for selecting the right business model and entity structure for the smooth flow of the entity’s business. Also we assist you with their registrations procedure in India.
